Bisq 2

What is new?

Bisq 2 is the new version of Bisq which comes with lots of improvements and more flexibility.
  • Multiple trade apps
  • Multiple privacy networks (Tor, I2P)
  • Multiple identities (create a new identity for each trade for better privacy)

Bisq Easy

Bisq Easy is the first trade protocol introduced in Bisq 2. It is specifically designed for individuals new to Bitcoin. Its primary aim is to assist newcomers in obtaining their first Bitcoin while providing a gentle and friendly introduction to the world of Bitcoin.. To reduce high transaction fees, Bitcoins can also be transferred using the Lightning Network.

Note: Bisq 2 is not replacing Bisq 1. So don't uninstall Bisq 1. At this point Bisq 2 comes with the first module called 'Bisq-Easy' only. You can install both in parallel.

Bisq Easy is for beginners. So if you are a seasoned user of Bisq, you probably won't have much benefit from using Bisq Easy. Except, if you want to be a seller on Bitcoin Easy and want to support beginners buying their first satoshis.
